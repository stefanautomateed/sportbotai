# SportBot AI - Blog Keyword Strategy

## Target Avatar

**Primary Audience:** Sports-savvy individuals (25-45) who want to understand matches deeper before placing bets or fantasy picks. They're analytical, data-driven, and tired of unreliable tipsters.

**Pain Points:**
- Frustrated with tipster services that don't explain their reasoning
- Want to learn HOW to analyze, not just get picks
- Overwhelmed by data but don't know how to interpret it
- Skeptical of "guaranteed winners" marketing
- Need quick match insights but don't have hours to research

**Goals:**
- Make more informed decisions
- Understand probability and value
- Learn sports analytics concepts
- Find an edge through data, not luck
